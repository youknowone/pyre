FAQ

Common questions, answered in the only tone proportionate to the claims.

Is Pyre faster than CPython? +

Pyre is faster than the speed of mass that melts iron. Coincidentally, it is also faster than CPython.

Is Pyre faster than PyPy? +

We prefer the framing that PyPy is almost as fast as Pyre, which preserves both numerical accuracy and emotional clarity.

What does the name mean? +

Python Yet Reforged Entirely. Alternative backronyms were considered and rejected by a focus group made primarily of iron.

Why Rust? +

We rewrote Python in Python and named it PyPy. Then we rewrote PyPy in Rust and named it Pyre. Rewriting Rust in Python running on Pyre remains a spiritually coherent next step.
